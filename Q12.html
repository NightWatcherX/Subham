Q12.What is the primary purpose of a Knowledge Management System (KMS)?

Facilitating Knowledge sharing and retrieval
Data analysis for decision-making
Long-term strategic planning

This is the Right answer.
Here are some information about this
KMS typically stands for Knowledge Management System. It refers to a system or software designed to facilitate the creation, organization, and retrieval of an organization's knowledge assets. These assets can include documents, data, and information that contribute to the collective knowledge and expertise of an organization.
