Q9.Which of the following is not a characteristic of a good MIS?

It is accurate and reliable
It is expensive and complex
It is flexible and adaptable

This is the Right answer.
Here are some information about this

A management information system (MIS) is a system that collects, organizes, analyzes, and presents information for decision-making and coordination in an organization. It involves people, processes, and technology in an organizational context. An MIS can help managers to improve operational efficiency, customer service, marketing, and strategic planning. Some examples of MIS are accounting systems, inventory systems, customer relationship management systems, and enterprise resource planning systems
